# Purpose

Motivation & Needs defines why intelligent agents choose to act.

While emotion determines how experiences are internally evaluated, motivation determines which goals become worth pursuing.

Every meaningful action within The Garden originates from one or more underlying needs.

Needs create motivation.

Motivation creates intention.

Intention creates action.

---

# Scientific Basis

Behavior is driven by multiple interacting motivational systems rather than a single universal objective.

Needs compete, reinforce one another, and change throughout an individual's lifetime.

The Garden models motivation as a dynamic hierarchy of continuously changing priorities influenced by biology, experience, personality, relationships, culture, and circumstance.

---

# Design Philosophy

No individual acts without reason.

The reason may be obvious.

The reason may be hidden.

The reason may even be misunderstood by the individual.

Every action has motivation.

Understanding those motivations is understanding the individual.
